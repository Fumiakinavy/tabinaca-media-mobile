## Quick Overview

Step into the **Sengoku era**—right in the heart of Shibuya.  
At **Samurai Armor Photo Studio**, professional dressers will outfit you in authentic armor, complete with *kabuto* helmet and *katana* sword.  
Choose between a **Studio Course (90 min)** for dramatic indoor shots or a **Street Course (120 min)** where you march through **Dogenzaka and Shibuya Crossing** as passersby cheer.  
All photos are handed to you on the same day.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1:** Select your favorite armor style—each modeled after different clans or historical periods  
- **Step 2:** Get fully dressed: *kosode* & *tattsuke-bakama* → *cuirass*, *kote*, *suneate*, and *kabuto*  
- **Step 3:** Pose with swords and spears across two studio sets (sitting and standing); expect around **10 poses / 170 shots**  
- **Step 4:** *(Street Course)* head outdoors to **Dogenzaka and Shibuya Crossing** for a cinematic photoshoot  
- **Step 5:** Receive your photo data on an **SD card the same day**
