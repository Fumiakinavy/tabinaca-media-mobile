## Quick Overview

Drop into a friendly board-game hub 3 minutes from Ikebukuro Station and play 2–4 easy, language-light games in 60–90 minutes. Choose either a simple Free-Time plan (flat ¥2,000 per person) or the 1-Play chip system (¥100–¥400 per game). Open late—perfect between sightseeing or after dinner.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in** at the counter and tell staff your vibe (party / 2-player / strategy-lite)
- **Pick 2–4 games** (10–30 min each) and rotate through—learn in 2 minutes, play in 10–20
- **Keep the pace casual**: snap a shelf-shot, swap winners, or replay your favorite
- **Wrap up** at 60–90 minutes (or extend with Free-Time)

## What's Included

- Seat at Board Game Cafe ONE with access to a large library of games
- Game suggestions tailored to time and group size
- Drink included with time packs

## Perfect For

- Travelers who want a low-language, social activity near Ikebukuro
- Couples or small groups
- Rainy-day plans; night owls (open until 25:00 most days)
