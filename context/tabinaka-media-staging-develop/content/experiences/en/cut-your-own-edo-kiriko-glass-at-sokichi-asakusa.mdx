## Quick Overview

Create an original Edo-kiriko cut-glass tumbler just 30 seconds from Asakusa Station. Choose a colored glass and pattern, learn the basics, then use a small cutting wheel to carve your design. English/Chinese support is available with advance notice. Take your finished glass home the same day; optional name engraving, gift boxing, or shipping available.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in** at the workshop near Kaminarimon and browse sample pieces for inspiration
- **Pick your glass** (color/shape) and a template, get a safety briefing, and practice lines on a trial piece
- **Sketch light guide lines**, then cut your motif using a hand-operated wheel with staff guidance
- **Rinse, check, and polish**; optional initials/short name can be added (fee)
- **Photograph your work** at the studio and pack it to take away—or arrange paid shipping

## What's Included

- 90-minute Edo-kiriko workshop session (120-minute course also available)
- Use of tools, practice piece, pattern templates, and safety gear
- One finished glass to take home same day
- Staff guidance; English/Chinese support when scheduled in advance

## Perfect For

- Travelers who want a hands-on, take-home Japanese craft in under two hours
- Couples, friends, or families with teens who enjoy creative activities
- Craft lovers seeking a uniquely Tokyo souvenir
