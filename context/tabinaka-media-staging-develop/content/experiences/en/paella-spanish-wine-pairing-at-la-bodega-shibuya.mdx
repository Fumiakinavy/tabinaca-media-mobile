## Quick Overview

Enjoy **authentic Spanish dining** in the heart of Shibuya at **Bar Español La Bodega**.  
In this **90-minute experience**, taste tapas, freshly prepared paella, and sip Spanish wines or sangria inside Hikarie's stylish 7th-floor restaurant.  
Perfect for couples, friends, or travelers who want to combine **culture, flavors, and fun** in Tokyo.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Begin with Spanish tapas such as *jamón*, *ajillo* (garlic shrimp), or seasonal specials  
- **Step 2**: Watch (and smell!) as a fresh **paella** is cooked and brought to your table  
- **Step 3**: Enjoy a **wine or sangria flight** — choose from Spanish *cava*, red/white wines, or fruit sangria  
- **Step 4**: Learn the background of each dish and wine from the menu (basic English support)  
- **Step 5**: Relax in a lively bar atmosphere that feels straight out of Barcelona  

## What's Included

- 90-min Spanish dining experience at Shibuya Hikarie  
- Selection of tapas & one paella to share (chef's choice or guest choice)  
- 1 glass of Spanish wine, cava, or sangria  
- English-friendly menu and staff support  
- Reserved table in a modern, casual setting
