## Quick Overview

A craft-beer focused standing bar and crowler shop in Oku-Shibuya.  
Pick your favorite from **18 taps** and enjoy it freshly poured as a **full 1 pint**.  
Casual vibe, **food bring-in allowed**, and English support available.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Browse the tap list (up to 18 varieties; styles change regularly) and pick your favorite.
- **Step 2**: Order your **1 pint** draft; staff will pour it fresh.
- **Step 3**: Enjoy at the counter (standing “kaku-uchi” style). Take your time—about 40 minutes.

## What's Included

- One pint of draft beer (your choice from the tap list)

## Perfect For

- Craft beer lovers exploring Shibuya
- After-work casual drinks
- Travelers seeking local taproom vibes
- Small groups or couples looking for a quick, tasty stop
