## Quick Overview

Step into Japan's most famous escape game brand, **SCRAP**, and challenge yourself in a **90-minute live adventure** in the heart of Shibuya. Solve puzzles, unlock secrets, and experience thrilling stories—from classic escape rooms to anime-themed missions and spy-style infiltration games. Perfect for groups of friends, families, or team-building.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Join a 90-minute game where you and your team must solve mysteries to escape  
- **Step 2**: Choose from different game styles  
  - **Escape Game**: A room-sized puzzle adventure or hall-size scenario with live characters  
  - **Infiltration Game**: Sneak, hide, and complete missions in an action-thriller setting  
- **Step 3**: Collaborate, think creatively, and immerse yourself in a cinematic Japanese storyline  
- **Step 4**: Enjoy multilingual support (English scenarios available for select titles)

## What's Included

- 90-minute live puzzle experience in Shibuya  
- Game materials and setup  
- Basic English support (depending on scenario)  
- Staff assistance and game introduction  

## Perfect For

- Friends and families seeking an unforgettable group challenge  
- Travelers wanting to experience Japan's original "real escape" game culture  
- Team-building, date nights, or anyone looking for a thrilling non-touristy activity in Tokyo
