## Quick Overview

Indulge in five soft, golden-brown obanyaki pancakes at the Tokachi Anko Sazae stall in Shibuya Tokyu Food Show, each packed with your choice of rich red bean paste, smooth white bean paste, or decadent cream.

- **Step 1**: Select your flavor mix—coarse red bean paste, white bean paste, or cream.
- **Step 2**: Watch as the shop’s skilled chef cooks each pancake fresh to achieve a perfectly fluffy texture.
- **Step 3**: Savor your warm obanyaki on the spot or take them to go for a delightful snack anywhere.
