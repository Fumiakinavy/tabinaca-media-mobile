## Quick Overview

Step into Owl Village Harajuku for a calm, self-guided 60-minute encounter with resident owls—right by Harajuku Station. Staff give a brief safety talk in basic English, then you'll pet (back of head only), hold an owl on a glove for photos, and learn gentle etiquette. Optional, staff-guided feeding may be offered at set times. Advance booking is recommended; walk-in slots are limited.

## What You'll Do

- **Arrive 10 minutes early**; hear the house rules and owl-friendly tips (EN support)
- **Enter the owl room** for hands-on time—pet gently, pose, and take non-flash photos
- **Hold an owl** on a falconry glove for your keepsake shot (included)
- **(Optional) Join a staff-guided feeding** if offered during your slot (paid add-on)

## What's Included

- 60-minute session (on the hour) with staff guidance, glove photo, and owl-room access
- Drinks available for purchase; feeding is a paid add-on when scheduled

## Perfect For

- Animal lovers, families with older kids, couples, and solo travelers looking for a uniquely Japanese, gentle wildlife encounter—right next to the Harajuku/Omotesando sights

## Need-to-Know

- **Duration & Start**: 60 minutes; sessions begin every hour. Last entry 18:00
- **Price (guide)**: Digital pre-purchase ticket from ¥2,000 (limited time; seat reservation required separately); standard walk-in around ¥3,500; feeding add-on ~¥500; drinks ¥500〜. Check the official page for current pricing and availability
- **Booking**: Reserve online up to ~1 month ahead; same-day is in-person if space remains. Phone inquiries 10:00–12:00 (JST)
- **Age Policy**: Under-16s must be with an adult. Children under 7 may not be permitted in the owl room (café area is available—confirm on the day). Policies can change; please check before visiting
- **Rules & Etiquette**: No flash; quiet voices; touch only the back of the head; follow staff instructions at all times for owl welfare
- **Access**: Owl Village Harajuku—4F Harajuku ATM Bldg., 1-21-15 Jingumae, Shibuya. 1–2 min walk from JR Harajuku/Meiji-Jingumae. Hours 12:00–19:00
