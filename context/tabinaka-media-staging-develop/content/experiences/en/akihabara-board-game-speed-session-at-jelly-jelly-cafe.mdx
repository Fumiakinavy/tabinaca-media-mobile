## Quick Overview

Drop into Japan's famous board-game café and play from a wall of 500+ titles in the heart of Akihabara. Staff can recommend beginner-friendly games, and many titles include English rules. Pay the café's time-pack (includes 1 drink), play for 60–90 minutes, and head out—perfect between sightseeing stops.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in** at JELLY JELLY CAFE Akihabara (open 13:00–23:00 daily) and choose a time-pack
- **Tell staff the vibe** you want (party / strategy / 2-player); they'll point you to easy picks with English rules
- **Play 1–2 games** for 60–90 minutes; use quick-start guides or look up rules on your phone
- **Snap a photo** with your winning team and check the shelf for your next favorite title

## What's Included

- Café entry via a time-pack (includes 1 soft drink)
- Unlimited access to the in-house game library during your stay
- Staff recommendations; many games have English rule sheets

## Perfect For

- Friends, couples, or solo travelers who want a cozy, screen-free break
- Families with teens (great on rainy days)
- Game lovers hunting Japanese titles with English rules
