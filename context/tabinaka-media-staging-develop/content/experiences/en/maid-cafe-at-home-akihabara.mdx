## Quick Overview

Step into Tokyo's most famous maid café for a compact, first-timer-friendly session. You'll be greeted with the classic "Welcome home, Master/Princess," learn the café rules, order a cute drink or meal, and (optionally) take a souvenir cheki instant photo—all within ~60 minutes.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in** at the Akihabara main building and hear system explained in simple English
- **Order at least one item** (drink/food); enjoy table service with the "moe-moe chant" and latte-art or omurice doodles
- **Follow maid-café etiquette** (no touching, no unsolicited photos of maids; food/interior photos OK)
- **(Optional) Take a cheki** instant photo with your maid as a keepsake

## What's Included

- Seat at at-home café (time-limited)
- Basic English guidance on how to order and house rules

## Perfect For

- First-time visitors who want a safe, authentic maid-café intro
- Friends/couples looking for a uniquely "only-in-Japan" pop-culture stop
- Travelers exploring Akihabara's Electric Town
