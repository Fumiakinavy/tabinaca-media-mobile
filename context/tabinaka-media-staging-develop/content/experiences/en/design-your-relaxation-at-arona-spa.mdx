## Quick Overview

Craft your own 30–90 minute relaxation session right off Shibuya Center-gai. Pick your focus (deep tissue, lymphatic oil, head/face, or legs), choose your pressure and aroma, soak in a warm foot bath, then unwind in a private room. Women-staffed salon; couples room available. Open noon–late (Sun to 23:00; other days to 04:00).

## What You'll Do

- **Tell the desk** your target areas & preferred pressure (basic English support)
- **Choose your style**: dry bodywork + stretch, lymphatic oil, head/face focus, or leg-slim focus
- **Begin with a complimentary warm foot bath** to prep muscles
- **Enjoy your treatment in a private room** (couples room available)
- **Finish with a drink** and float back to Shibuya recharged

## What's Included

- Warm foot bath before treatment (all courses)
- Your selected treatment (30–90 min)
- Private room; couples layout on request
- Post-treatment drink

## Perfect For

- Jet-lagged travelers needing a quick reset between sights
- Couples/friends looking for a mellow break steps from Shibuya Crossing
- Creators/remote workers with neck–shoulder or eye fatigue
