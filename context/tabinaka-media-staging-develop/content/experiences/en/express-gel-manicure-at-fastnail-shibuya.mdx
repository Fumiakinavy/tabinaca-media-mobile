## Quick Overview

Pop into FASTNAIL Shibuya—1 minute from Shibuya Station (Miyamasuzaka exit)—choose from 160+ colors and ~700 design samples, and leave with shiny gel nails in as little as 30–45 minutes (one-color). Removal is free with reapplication, pricing is clear, and same-day booking is easy.

## What You'll Do

- **Pick your look**: one-color, French, magnet/cat-eye, or a simple preset design. Visual menus make choosing fast
- **Nail prep & gel application** by a pro technician; you relax
- **Optional add-ons** (a few accent parts or art) if time allows

## What's Included

- Gel manicure service (hand or foot)
- Free removal when changing designs at this visit (and on future visits, per their "off free" policy)
- Clear, upfront pricing with time-saving preset menus

## Perfect For

- Travelers with a tight schedule, last-minute event touch-ups, or anyone wanting trend-forward nails near Shibuya Crossing without spending hours

## Need-to-Know

- **Where**: FPG Shibuya Miyamasu Bldg. 5F, 1-24-15 Shibuya, Shibuya-ku (1 min from Miyamasuzaka exit)
- **Hours (typical)**: Weekdays 12:00–22:00; Sat/Sun/Holiday 10:00–20:00. (Check your slot when booking.)
- **Sample Prices (tax-incl., promos vary)**:
  - One-color / magnet one-color from ¥3,850 (off free)
  - "Simple set" preset design from ¥4,860 (off free)
  - Foot one-color or simple design from ¥5,280 (weekday promo)
- **Time & Menu Ideas**:
  - 30–45 min: Hand one-color (great between sights)
  - 45–60 min: Hand simple preset design (e.g., French or subtle art)
  - 45–60 min: Foot one-color/simple (fast if no removal)
  - 60–75 min: One-color + 2–4 accent nails/parts (ask availability at check-in)
- **Booking Tips**: Reserve online (Hot Pepper/official) for peak times; walk-ins are possible when seats free. Look for weekday midday discounts. If you already have gel on, mention it when booking—removal is free with a new application, but it affects timing
