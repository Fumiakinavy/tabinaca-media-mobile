## Quick Overview

Step into **Alain Ducasse's Tokyo Manufacture in Nihonbashi**—Japan's flagship **bean-to-bar chocolate atelier**.  
Sip and sample in the upstairs Salon while viewing chocolatiers at work, then browse the boutique for gift boxes crafted from single-origin cacao.  
Select days also offer **dessert and afternoon tea-style service**, making this a refined 45–90 minute chocolate immersion.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Settle into the **mezzanine Salon** overlooking the chocolate workshop  
- **Step 2**: Enjoy a guided tasting menu of **desserts and drinks**, available seasonally  
- **Step 3**: Taste through **origin chocolates** highlighting terroir and craft, from roasting to tempering  
- **Step 4 (Optional)**: Upgrade to the Salon's **signature sweets**—hot chocolate or plated desserts  
- **Step 5**: Visit the **boutique downstairs** to curate your own gift box of ganaches, pralinés, or bars  

## What's Included

- Seat in the Salon (factory-view) with English-friendly introduction  
- Tasting flight or dessert/drink menu (varies by day)  
- Information sheet with chocolate origins and tasting notes  
- Time to shop at the boutique after your session  
- Typical hours: Boutique 11:00–20:00; Salon Sun–Thu 11:00–18:00 (L.O. 17:30), Fri–Sat 11:00–19:00 (L.O. 18:30)
