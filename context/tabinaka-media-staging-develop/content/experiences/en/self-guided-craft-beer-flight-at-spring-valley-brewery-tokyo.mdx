## Quick Overview

Sip your way through six distinctive Japanese craft beers at Spring Valley Brewery's flagship in Daikanyama. Order the popular "Flight 6" (6 × 100 ml), follow the tasting mat, and discover signatures like 496, Afterdark, Daydream, and Jazzberry. Indoor tanks-on-view and an airy terrace make this an easy, walk-in mini-adventure just off Shibuya.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in** at the 1F bar and order the Flight 6—a curated six-beer sampler
- **Use the tasting mat** to move light → dark and note your favorites for a full-size pint later
- **(Optional) Pair** with a snack—sausages or a starter board are easy wins
- **Wrap up** with photos by the glass-encased kettles or on the terrace

## What's Included

- Beer Flight 6 (six recommended SVB beers × 100 ml) - on-site purchase
- Optional add-ons: food pairings, additional pints, or course menus

## Perfect For

- Travelers who want a 30–60 min taste tour near Shibuya (low effort, high flavor)
- Beer fans comparing Japanese styles side-by-side
- Couples & friends seeking a casual pre-dinner stop
