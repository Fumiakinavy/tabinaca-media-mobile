## Quick Overview

At STYLE OF LAB, immerse yourself in a stylish, fragrance-filled space where you can design your own fountain pen. Choose from over 6 million possible color combinations to create a thoughtful gift for yourself or someone special. No reservation is required — just walk in and start crafting.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Choose your favorite colors for each of the five pen parts from about 20 color options per part.
- **Step 2**: Assemble your selected parts with guidance from staff to ensure a perfect fit.
- **Step 3**: Package your completed pen in an elegant gift box; one cartridge ink is included.

## What's Included

- Custom-designed fountain pen
- Cartridge ink
- Special gift box packaging
