## Quick Overview

Right inside **Shibuya Hikarie's 7th floor**, discover **SIZZLE GAZZLE**, a café-grill where **Japanese wagyu beef** meets **Western comfort food**.  
In this **60–90 minute experience**, enjoy a signature hamburg steak with your choice of sauce, pair it with wine or craft beer, and finish with fluffy French toast or a seasonal dessert set.  
Perfect for lunch, dinner, or an indulgent café break between shopping and sightseeing.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Choose a hand-crafted **hamburg steak** (wagyu or beef blend, 300g double option available) with your choice of sauce from 12 styles (*cheese*, *demiglace*, *garlic butter*, and more)  
- **Step 2**: Pair with side dishes and drinks — wine, cocktails, or premium draft beer poured "神泡" style  
- **Step 3**: For dessert, enjoy **signature French toast** or a **seasonal fruit-based dessert set**  
- **Step 4**: Relax in a stylish yet casual dining space overlooking Shibuya—perfect for friends, couples, or solo travelers  
- **Step 5 (Optional)**: Add a celebration plate for birthdays or anniversaries  

## What's Included

- Choice of hamburg steak set (wagyu or beef blend)  
- Seasonal dessert or French toast  
- 1 drink (coffee/tea, soft drink, or alcohol)  
- Comfortable seating in a modern café-grill setting (50 seats, semi-private space available)
