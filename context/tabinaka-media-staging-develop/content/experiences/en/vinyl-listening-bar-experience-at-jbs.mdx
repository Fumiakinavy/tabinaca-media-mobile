## Quick Overview

Sip a whisky and sink into pure analog sound at JBS—a tiny, legendary vinyl bar where the owner-DJ handpicks jazz, blues, and soul from a wall of records. Speak softly, listen deeply, and savor a quintessential Tokyo "jazz kissa" moment.

## What You'll Do

- **Slide onto a bar stool** and let Kobayashi-san cue LPs from his vast collection; album jackets are displayed so you can follow what's playing
- **Order a simple drink** (think whisky or beer) and enjoy a curated, vinyl-only set in an intimate room built for listening
- **Embrace the vibe**: low voices, no loud talk—this is a place for music-first conversation

## What's Included

- Your seat at the counter (first-come, first-served)
- Owner-curated vinyl listening session
- Drinks are pay-as-you-go on site

## Perfect For

- Solo travelers, couples, and music lovers who want a quiet, authentic Tokyo vinyl-bar experience
- Jazz, blues, and soul enthusiasts
- Travelers seeking a screen-free, meditative break in Shibuya
