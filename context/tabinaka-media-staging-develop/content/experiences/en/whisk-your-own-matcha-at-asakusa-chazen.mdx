## Quick Overview

Step into an authentic tatami tearoom just off Kaminarimon Gate and learn the essentials of chanoyu—then whisk and serve matcha yourself. Staff give a short English introduction and demo; after that, you'll follow simple cue cards to prepare, present, and enjoy your tea with a seasonal sweet.

## What You'll Do

- **Enter a traditional chashitsu** with nijiriguchi entry and tsukubai basin; settle on tatami
- **Hear a concise English briefing** on tea tools, etiquette, and the flow of the ceremony (5–7 min)
- **Watch a short demonstration**, then whisk your own bowl of matcha using step-by-step cards
- **Pair your tea** with a traditional wagashi sweet and practice a simple guest-host exchange

## What's Included

- Use of tearoom, utensils, and premium matcha
- Seasonal wagashi (one per person)
- English support by tea staff (intro + light assistance)

## Perfect For

- First-time visitors, couples, families, and small groups who want a hands-on, time-efficient cultural experience in Asakusa
- Anyone seeking an authentic tea ceremony without a lengthy commitment
- Travelers wanting to learn traditional Japanese customs
