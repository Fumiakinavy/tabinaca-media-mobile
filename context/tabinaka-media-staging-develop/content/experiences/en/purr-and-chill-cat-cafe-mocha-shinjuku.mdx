## Quick Overview

Unwind with friendly cats in central Shinjuku. Drop in for 30–90 minutes, sip from the included drink bar, time your visit for the adorable "Meal Time" (10:30 & 19:00), and (optionally) treat the kitties. Walk-in welcome—no reservation needed. Ages 13+ only at this branch.

## What You'll Do

- **Settle into the airy lounge**, meet the resident cats, relax, read, or take photos (no flash)
- **Time it for Meal Time** (10:30 / 19:00) to see everyone line up and munch together—peak cuteness
- **Enjoy the included drink bar** throughout your stay
- **(Optional) Buy cat treats** (¥550) on site to make new furry friends

## What's Included

- Entry for your chosen time (30–90 minutes, self-guided)
- Unlimited soft-drink bar
- Wi-Fi / some workspace-friendly seating zones
- Staff on hand to ensure cat comfort and guest guidance

## Perfect For

- Solo travelers needing a calm reset between Shinjuku sights
- Couples & friends looking for a cozy photo-worthy stop
- Cat lovers planning light "gap-time" (30–60 min)
