## Quick Overview

In 45–75 minutes, enjoy a seasonal set meal or dessert at MEALS (2F) served on Marumitsu Poterie plates, then pop downstairs to DISHES (1F) to "hunt" the exact tableware you just used. Staff provide a brief English explanation; you leave with photos, a favorite plate (optional purchase), and a sharper eye for Japanese table styling.

**Where/When:** MEALS ARE DELIGHTFUL (DISHES 1F shop + MEALS 2F café), 1-17-5 Tomigaya, Shibuya. 8 min from Yoyogi-Koen / Yoyogi-Hachiman. Hours: DISHES 11:00–19:00; MEALS lunch 11:30–17:00, dessert & drinks from 14:30 (weekends from 15:00). Closed Wed. MEALS reservations: only the 11:30 opening slot is bookable; otherwise walk-in.

## What You'll Do

- **Order & Taste (20–40 min)**: At MEALS (2F), choose the seasonal "Monthly Meals" set or a dessert & drink. Note the plate/bowl names on the menu cards or ask staff (basic EN)
- **Photo & Style (5–10 min)**: Snap your plating—MEALS intentionally showcases Studio M'/SOBOKAI ware with seasonal food styling
- **Tableware Hunt (15–20 min)**: Head to DISHES (1F) and find the exact items you used; browse ~1,500 pieces arranged by theme and season
- **Optional Add-On (5 min)**: Pick up your favorite plate/cup to take home, or just window-shop—no purchase required

## What's Included

- Self-guided activity map (these steps)
- Café seating at MEALS (order separately) and access to the 1F tableware showroom
- Basic English support from staff (ordering/product names)

## Perfect For

- Design lovers, foodies, couples, solo travelers; rainy-day plans near Yoyogi Park; anyone curious about Japanese tableware & plating

## Need-to-Know

- **Price Guide (pay as you go)**: MEALS set/dessert & drink typically ¥1,000–¥1,999 at the café; shop items priced individually
- **Closed**: Wednesdays
- **Café location**: 2F of the same building as the shop (1F); arrive a few minutes before 11:30 if you want a bookable slot
- **Concept**: The concept ties food with plates—expect seasonal, home-style dishes and thoughtful plating
