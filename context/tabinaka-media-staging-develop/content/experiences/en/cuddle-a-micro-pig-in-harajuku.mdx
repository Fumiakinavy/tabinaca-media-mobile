## Quick Overview

Meet ultra-friendly micro pigs at mipig cafe in Harajuku. Book a 25- or 55-minute slot, settle onto low sofas, and let the pigs climb into your lap for the ultimate stress-relief moment. Advanced online booking only; photo/video is welcome (no flash). Two central locations: Harajuku and Harajuku Takeshita Street.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in & sanitize** at reception (reservation required; walk-ins not accepted). Staff will seat you.
- **Meet the herd** in a cozy lounge; stretch your legs—pigs may gently step onto you. (Please don't pick them up.)
- **Capture the moment** with as many photos/videos as you like (just no flash/live streaming).
- **Unwind** as the pigs rotate between guests; feel free to book back-to-back slots if you want longer cuddle time.

## What's Included

- Reserved 25 or 55 minutes in the lounge with micro pigs
- Seating area and gentle guidance on how to interact
- Permission to take photos/videos (no flash)

## Perfect For

- Solo travelers, couples, families with kids seeking a calm, Instagrammable break in central Tokyo
- Animal lovers looking for a unique Japanese experience
- Travelers wanting a weather-proof activity near Harajuku
