## Quick Overview

Step into Owlpark – Owl Café Ikebukuro West Exit and spend 30–60 minutes getting up close to gentle owls. Hold them on a handler's glove, try a short flight-to-hand moment, and (optionally) feed them. Hedgehogs and a few small reptiles are also on site.

## What You'll Do

- **Receive a brief safety talk**, then hold an owl on a glove (staff assist)
- **Try an owl flight-to-hand** photo moment (staff-guided)
- **Optional feeding** (small cup, +¥300)
- **Meet other residents** (e.g., hedgehogs, iguana/white snake) and take photos

## What's Included

- Entry & owl handling (hand-perch)
- Staff assistance & basic guidance
- Free time to take photos (non-flash recommended)
- Optional add-on: Owl feeding cup (+¥300)

## Perfect For

- Animal lovers, families, couples, and solo travelers seeking a unique, relaxing mini-experience
- Travelers on the JR line between Shinjuku/Shibuya and Ikebukuro
- Anyone wanting a calm break from shopping and sightseeing
