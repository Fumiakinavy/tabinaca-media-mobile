## Quick Overview

Glide through old Tokyo in a hand-pulled rickshaw from Asakusa's Kaminarimon Gate. Choose 15–90 minutes, snap photos at Senso-ji's best angles, and enjoy simple English support from your puller. Same-day walk-ups are common; online booking available.

## What You'll Do

- **Meet by Kaminarimon** and hop into a 1–2-seat rickshaw (kids under 6 may ride with an adult)
- **Cruise Nakamise Street** side lanes and temple grounds; request stops for photos at Senso-ji, Five-Story Pagoda, or Skytree viewpoints
- **Hear quick local tips** from your puller (basic English/Korean/Chinese supported)
- **Pick your duration**: 15–20 min "Sampler," 30–45 min "Highlights," 60–90 min "Deep Dive" (Sumida riverside & hidden alleys)

## What's Included

- Private rickshaw ride (1–2 guests) with multilingual basic support
- Photo stops on request
- Route suggestions (temple, retro streets, Skytree views)

## Perfect For

- Travelers who want iconic Tokyo photos fast
- Families/couples seeking a weather-flexible, low-effort outing
- First-timers pairing Asakusa with Ueno/Akihabara in the same day
