## Quick Overview

Drop into a friendly neighborhood vintage shop five minutes from Yoyogi-Koen Station and speed-run a curated rack of European mens & womens pieces—lots of Euro work/military, soft tailoring, and one-of-a-kind finds. Name literally means "friends," and that's the vibe. Open 13:00–20:00, irregular holidays.

## What You'll Do

- **Warm-Up Scan (5 min)**: Do a quick lap to spot your theme—olive drab, French work blue, or soft neutrals
- **3-Item Styling Sprint (20–30 min)**: Pull 2 tops + 1 bottom (or outerwear) and build a look; mirror-test fit and comfort
- **Provenance Check (5–10 min)**: Hunt for clues (labels, language stamps, hardware) to guess decade & origin—many pieces are Euro or Euro-military
- **Keep/Pass (5 min)**: Photograph each option (ask staff before shooting), then decide what comes with you
- **Exit Shot (2 min)**: Street-style photo outside; Yoyogi Park is a short stroll for your outfit pic

## What's Included

- A self-guided mini-mission with on-floor browsing; friendly staff can help with sizing or brand hints when available
- Shop also posts select pieces online

## Perfect For

- Solo thrifters, couples, and creators who want a quick, photogenic vintage fix near Shibuya without a guide

## Need-to-Know

- **Hours**: 13:00–20:00, irregular holidays; check IG stories before you go
- **Focus**: European vintage across men's & women's, with notable Euro military/workwear
- **Address & Access**: Sunrise Tomigaya 102, 1-18-3 Tomigaya, Shibuya 151-0063. ~5 min walk from Yoyogi-Koen (Chiyoda Line) / Yoyogi-Hachiman (Odakyu). Okushibu area
- **Etiquette**: Ask staff before indoor photos; be gentle with delicate fabrics; backpacks to the front in tight aisles
