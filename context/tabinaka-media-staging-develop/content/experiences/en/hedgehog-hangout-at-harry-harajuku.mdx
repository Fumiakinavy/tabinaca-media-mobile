## Quick Overview

Meet, gently handle, and photograph adorable hedgehogs at HARRY Harajuku Station Front Store—just a 1-minute walk from Harajuku Station. Walk-ins are welcome (waits can occur), or reserve a 60-minute slot. Vending-machine drinks are available; non-alcoholic BYO is allowed. From ¥1,430 per person for 30 minutes.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in**, sanitize hands, and get a quick safety briefing (English on-site)
- **Settle into a cozy, Brooklyn-style space** and choose a hedgehog to gently hold or watch. Some will even "ask" for snacks
- **Snapshot your spiky new friend** and enjoy a calm, screen-free reset
- **(Optional) Light snacks** for hedgehogs may be available on site; staff will advise

## What's Included

- 30 minutes in the animal area (60+ minutes if booking online)
- Use of handling/sanitation supplies; staff oversight focused on animal safety

## Perfect For

- Couples, friends, families with kids (12 and under must be with a parent), solo travelers, animal lovers, rainy-day plans, and anyone seeking a cute, uniquely Japanese memory near Meiji-Jingū/Harajuku

## Need-to-Know

- **Location**: 1-13-21 Jingumae, Shibuya-ku, Tokyo (1 min from Harajuku Sta.)
- **Hours**: Daily 11:00–19:00 (last check-in 18:30)
- **Price**: ¥1,430 / 30 min per person (tax incl.). Reservations require a 60-min minimum; same-day cancellation is non-refundable
- **Animals here**: Primarily hedgehogs (also hamsters, meerkats, an owl, guinea pig on site). Please confirm availability on arrival
- **Food & Drink**: No meals served; vending-machine drinks available. Non-alcoholic BYO permitted
- **Rules & Safety**: Sanitize hands before contact; follow staff guidance to avoid stressing animals. No pets may be brought into the café. Children under 12 must be accompanied by a parent
- **How to Use This as a Bookable "Self-Activity"**:
  - 15–30 min express: Pop in for a quick hedgehog meet-and-greet before/after Takeshita-dori
  - 60 min standard (recommended): Reserve online to avoid waits, especially weekends/holidays, and enjoy an unhurried session
