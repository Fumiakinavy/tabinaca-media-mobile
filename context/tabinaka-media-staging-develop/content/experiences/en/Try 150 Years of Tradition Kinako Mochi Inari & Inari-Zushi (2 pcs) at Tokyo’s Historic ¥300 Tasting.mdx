## Quick Overview

At Sagamiya, established in 1869, enjoy two iconic inari-age varieties: sweet Kinako Mochi Inari and classic Inari-zushi. Each is made with seasoned tofu pouches steeped in over 150 years of Tokyo culinary tradition.

## What You’ll Do

- **Step 1**: Choose your inari option:
  - Kinako Mochi Inari (¥300)
  - Inari-zushi (2 pieces, ¥300)
- **Step 2**: Watch as the staff prepare and season each tofu pouch by hand.
- **Step 3**: Savor the fluffy exterior and rich filling that reflect Sagamiya’s historic recipe.

## What's Included

- One Kinako Mochi Inari **OR** two pieces of Inari-zushi

## Perfect For

- Snack lovers craving authentic Tokyo treats
- Travelers curious about traditional Japanese flavors
- History buffs interested in long-standing culinary techniques
- Anyone seeking a quick, delicious bite on the go
