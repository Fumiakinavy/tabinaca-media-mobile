## Quick Overview

Shape, texture, and hand-stamp your own silver or gold ring at nane tokyo Cat Street in Harajuku/Omotesandō. Staff provide simple English support, you do the crafting, and you take your ring home the same day. Tax-free available.

## What You'll Do

- **Choose your metal** (silver or gold), width, and finish
- **Learn basic tools**, then hammer and form the band yourself (staff guide lightly in English)
- **Personalize** with hand-stamped engraving (letters/numbers/symbols; even a Cat-Street-only "東京/Tokyo" stamp option)
- **Final sizing & polishing**; wear it out right away

## What's Included

- 70-minute bench time & tool use
- Staff assistance (basic English)
- Same-day finishing & takeaway box

## Perfect For

- Couples, friends, solo travelers wanting a hands-on, customizable souvenir
- Jewelry enthusiasts looking for a unique Tokyo experience
- Travelers seeking a creative break between shopping in Harajuku/Omotesandō
