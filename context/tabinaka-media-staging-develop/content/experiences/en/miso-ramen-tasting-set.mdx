## Quick Overview

At Izobachi, savor half-portions of three ramen styles—flame-charred miso ramen, Sapporo-style miso, and your choice of Chuka soba or Ume tsukemen—paired with a handmade rice ball for a perfectly balanced tasting experience.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Choose your set:
  - **A Set**: Izobachi miso + Sapporo-style miso + rice ball
  - **B Set**: Izobachi miso + Chuka soba + rice ball
  - **C Set**: Izobachi miso + Ume tsukemen + rice ball
- **Step 2**: Sample each half-portion ramen, comparing rich, smoky miso to lighter broths.
- **Step 3**: Enjoy the rice ball to round out the flavors.

## What's Included

- Three half-size bowls of ramen (Izobachi miso + 2 styles of your choice)
- Handmade rice ball
