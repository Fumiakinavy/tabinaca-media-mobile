## Quick Overview

Step into a studio just a 2-minute walk from Shinjuku Marui Annex for an unlimited photoshoot. Capture creativity and fun with up to 2 people; toddlers are free. Basic English assistance with lighting and camera settings included. Choose from 15-minute express or 30-minute standard sessions.

## What You'll Do

- **Arrive** and choose your backdrop or scene in the Shinjuku studio
- **Take as many photos** as you like for your chosen time (15 or 30 minutes)
- **Staff helps** with lighting and camera settings (simple English support)
- **Use the on-site changing area** to switch outfits if you like

## What's Included

- Photo session (15 or 30 minutes) for up to 2 people
- Toddlers (pre-school age) join free of charge
- All digital photo files (same-day handover, when possible)
- Simple English assistance from staff
- Access to studio backdrops, props, and changing room

## Perfect For

- Couples or friends in Shinjuku seeking a fun keepsake
- Tourists wanting high-quality Instagram-worthy portraits
- Travelers with a small time-gap between shopping and dinner
