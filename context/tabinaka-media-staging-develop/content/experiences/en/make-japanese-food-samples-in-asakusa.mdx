## Quick Overview

Create hyper-realistic Japanese "food samples" at a long-running workshop in Kappabashi, Asakusa. In about 60 minutes, staff demonstrate traditional wax techniques so you can craft two tempura pieces and a crisp head of "lettuce." No experience needed; kid-friendly; take your creations home the same day.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in** and get a quick safety and technique briefing
- **Make wax tempura**—learn the pour, dip, and shape methods to achieve realistic batter texture
- **Make a wax lettuce**—pour and fold warm wax sheets to form layered leaves
- **Cool, trim, and pack** your pieces; enjoy photo time and browse the shop for souvenirs

## What's Included

- 60-minute hands-on session
- All wax, tools, and protective gear (apron/gloves)
- Step-by-step staff guidance (basic EN support)
- Packaging to take your items home

## Perfect For

- Families and first-time visitors seeking a uniquely Japanese craft
- Couples/friends looking for a fun, quick, indoor activity near Sensō-ji
- DIY lovers and content creators who want eye-catching, Tokyo-made souvenirs
