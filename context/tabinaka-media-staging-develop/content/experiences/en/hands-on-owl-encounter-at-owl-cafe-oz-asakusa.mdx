## Quick Overview

Meet, gently handle, and photograph real owls at a cozy animal café just minutes from Senso-ji. A 45–60 minute self-guided visit includes a welcome briefing (basic English support), one drink, glove use, and supervised photo time with several owl species—plus other small animals on site.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in & sanitize**; staff give safety rules (no flash, calm voices, slow movements)
- **Choose your drink** (1 included) and settle in
- **Meet the owls** from small to large—touch and hold (with glove) under staff supervision; take plenty of photos
- **Optional**: say hi to other café animals like hedgehogs, rabbits, and more when available

## What's Included

- 45–60 min café visit (self-guided with staff supervision)
- 1 soft drink
- Protective glove use for handling owls
- Basic English assistance for rules and photos

## Perfect For

- Families, couples, small friend groups looking for a unique, animal-friendly memory
- Travelers combining Senso-ji / Nakamise with a nearby, weather-proof activity
- Content creators seeking gentle, photogenic moments
