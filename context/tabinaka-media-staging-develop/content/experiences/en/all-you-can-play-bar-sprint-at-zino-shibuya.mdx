## Quick Overview

Jump into a flexible, time-charge system: pay in 30-minute blocks, enjoy all-you-can-drink, and bounce between karaoke, darts, and casual games. Open late and ideal for a quick 30–90 min stop between plans. Basic English support.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in**, choose your stay length (extendable in 30-min increments)
- **Order unlimited drinks** during your session
- **Play freely**: switch between karaoke booths, darts, and simple bar games
- **Settle up** at the counter or extend if you're having fun

## What's Included

- Time-based all-you-can-drink plan (30-min units)
- Free play on darts & karaoke (first-come access)
- Basic English assistance from staff

## Perfect For

- Friends/couples wanting a fast, high-energy night activity near Shibuya Station
- Solo travelers filling a 30–90 min gap after dinner or shopping
- Night owls and last-train-missers who want low-commitment fun
