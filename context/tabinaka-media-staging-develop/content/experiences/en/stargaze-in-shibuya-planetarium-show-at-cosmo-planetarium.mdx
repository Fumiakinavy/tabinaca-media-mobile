## Quick Overview

Swap the scramble for the cosmos at **Cosmo Planetarium Shibuya**, a modern **17-meter dome** with 120 seats located on the **12th floor of Shibuya Cultural Center Owada**.  
Shows range from live constellation talks to music-led programs and science features.  
Book tickets online, sit back, and enjoy 45–60 minutes of calm, star-filled escape just minutes from Shibuya Station.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Choose a **45–60 min program**—options include *Tonight's Stars*, *Kids Time*, music shows, and seasonal or astronomy specials  
- **Step 2**: Lean back under the **high-resolution dome** as the city glow fades into deep space  
- **Step 3**: Enjoy a guided narration (Japanese) or immersive visual storytelling—language isn't needed to feel the experience  
- **Step 4**: After the show, explore nearby cafés or night views just a few steps away from the venue  

## What's Included

- Admission to one scheduled **planetarium program** (17-m dome, 120-seat theater)  
- **Live guide** or **curated soundtrack**, depending on the session  
- Optional online booking (tickets available up to 5 days in advance)  

## Perfect For

- Couples, friends, and families needing a **quiet 1-hour reset** in the middle of Shibuya  
- **Space and photography fans** seeking a weather-proof stargazing experience  
- **Parents with children** (choose Kids Time slots)
