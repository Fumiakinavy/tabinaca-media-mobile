## Quick Overview

Step into **Uobei Shibuya Dogenzaka**, a **futuristic conveyor-belt sushi** spot where delicious plates speed to your seat in seconds. In just **30–60 minutes**, enjoy fresh sushi starting from **¥150 per plate**, along with ramen, chawanmushi, and sides. A perfect Tokyo *gap-time* activity for families, couples, or solo travelers.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Use a tablet (with English menu) to order sushi, ramen, or side dishes  
- **Step 2**: Watch as your sushi arrives on a **high-speed "sushi train"** straight to your seat  
- **Step 3**: Taste as many plates as you like, mixing traditional favorites with seasonal specials  
- **Step 4**: Try extras like **miso soup** or **desserts** for a full Japanese casual dining experience  
- **Step 5**: Pay easily with credit card or IC card before heading back to explore Shibuya  

## What's Included

- 30–60 min sushi dining experience  
- English menu & tablet ordering system  
- Access to 100+ sushi and side dish options  
- Family-friendly seating (121 seats available)  
- Affordable pricing (¥150 per plate and up)  

## Perfect For

- Tourists wanting a fun, quick, and affordable Japanese sushi experience  
- Friends and couples looking for a casual Shibuya food stop  
- Families with kids (child-friendly seating and menu)  
- Solo travelers needing a fast but authentic Japanese meal
