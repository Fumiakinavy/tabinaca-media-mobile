## Quick Overview

Step inside a real ninja house hidden in Shinjuku and learn the secrets of Japan's legendary spies. In this 40-minute experience, you'll explore ninja tools, throw real shuriken (ninja stars), try sword techniques, and discover secret tricks inside a traditional-style hideout. Friendly staff provide guidance with simple English support. Perfect for families, couples, and travelers looking for a fun cultural adventure in Tokyo.

## What You'll Do

- **Enter the ninja hideout** and see trap doors and trick walls
- **Learn the history** of ninjas and their tools
- **Practice shuriken throwing** (ninja stars) under safe supervision
- **Try out a katana** (sword) and learn basic ninja moves
- **Take memorable photos** in the ninja setting

## What's Included

- 40-minute guided experience in a ninja trick house
- Shuriken throwing & ninja sword trial
- Simple English guidance and explanations
- Photo opportunities inside the ninja house
- All equipment (shuriken, practice sword, props) provided

## Perfect For

- Families with kids (age 2+ with guardian)
- Couples looking for a unique Tokyo memory
- Travelers who want a short but authentic cultural activity between sightseeing in Shinjuku
