## Quick Overview

Slip into a kimono or samurai outfit and capture studio-quality portraits in Asakusa. Dressing is handled by staff; you then self-pose for a rapid shoot. All photo data is handed over on the spot (SD card)—perfect between temple visits.

## What You'll Do

- **Choose your look** (kimono, hakama, or samurai style) from the studio wardrobe
- **Get dressed by pros**, then step into a simple studio set
- **Use pose samples** to self-direct; staff help with basic cues and shutter
- **Receive all images** on an SD card immediately

## What's Included

- Costume & props rental
- Studio use
- Basic posing support
- Photo data on SD card (one per party)
- Hair set available as an add-on

## Perfect For

- Solo travelers, couples, friends, and families wanting a fast, iconic keepsake near Sensō-ji
- Travelers seeking unique souvenir photos without a long photoshoot
- Anyone wanting professional-quality images in traditional Japanese attire
