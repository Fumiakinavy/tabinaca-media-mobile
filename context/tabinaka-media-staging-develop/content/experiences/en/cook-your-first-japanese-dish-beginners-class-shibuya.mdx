## Quick Overview

Never cooked before? This hands-on cooking class is designed **especially for beginners**. In **90 minutes**, you'll learn step-by-step how to prepare a simple Japanese home dish—from knife handling to seasoning—and complete the recipe entirely on your own. No prior experience needed; staff provide friendly, basic English support.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Receive your own cooking station with knife, cutting board, and utensils  
- **Step 2**: Learn the basics: how to hold a knife, measure ingredients, and set up your workspace  
- **Step 3**: Prepare one full Japanese dish (e.g., *miso soup*, *teriyaki chicken*, or a seasonal recipe)  
- **Step 4**: Practice the art of seasoning with professional tips—taste, adjust, and check balance  
- **Step 5**: Finish by plating your dish beautifully, then enjoy your handmade meal  

## What's Included

- 90-minute cooking session in Shibuya  
- All ingredients, utensils, and cooking station (one per person)  
- Guidance from a professional chef (basic English available)  
- Recipe notes and seasoning tips to take home  
- Your own finished dish to enjoy at the end  

## Perfect For

- Travelers who want to try cooking Japanese food for the first time  
- Couples, friends, or solo travelers seeking a fun, cultural hands-on activity  
- Food lovers interested in learning not just recipes but fundamental cooking skills  
- Anyone with a free morning or afternoon looking for a short, authentic experience in Shibuya
