## Quick Overview

Right off **Shibuya's Scramble Crossing**, **#C-pla Shibuya Center-gai** is a colorful, three-floor **capsule-toy world** featuring over **2,000 gachapon machines**.  
Explore themed "planets" filled with anime, food miniatures, and retro collectibles, then **assemble and photograph** your haul at the in-store **C-pit mini studio**.  
Open daily until 23:00—perfect for families, collectors, or anyone chasing unique Japanese souvenirs.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1:** Browse across three themed floors packed with gachapon machines  
- **Step 2:** Spin 3–5 machines (or more!) to collect your favorites  
- **Step 3:** Open and assemble your minis at the **C-pit lighted table**  
- **Step 4:** Snap creative photos at the **space-themed photo zones**  
- **Step 5:** Swap duplicates or challenge friends to themed "mini hunts"  

## What's Included

- Access to **2,000+ capsule machines** across three floors  
- Use of **C-pit assembly & mini photo studio**  
- In-store photo spots and display setups  
- *(Capsules are pay-as-you-go at each machine)*  

## Perfect For

- **Families** and **friends** looking for a quick, fun stop between sights  
- **Collectors** and **anime/figure fans** seeking rare or limited items  
- **Photographers** wanting colorful, creative capsule shots  

## Need-to-Know

- **Hours:** 10:00–23:00  
- **Address:** B1F/1F/2F, Shibuya Heiwa Bldg., 21-8 Udagawacho  
- **Access:** 1 min from Hachikō Exit, inside Center-gai arcade  
- **Tips:** Set a budget—some limited lines sell out fast. Dispose of packaging properly.  
- **No reservation needed:** walk in anytime; expect lively crowds
