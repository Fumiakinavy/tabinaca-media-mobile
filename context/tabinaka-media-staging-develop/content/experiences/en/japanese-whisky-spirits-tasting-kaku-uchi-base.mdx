## Quick Overview

Slip into a true Tokyo casual: a **standing bar ("kaku-uchi")** by the Shibuya River at **Shibuya Stream 1F**.  
In this **30–60 minute tasting stop**, build your own mini flight—Japanese whisky, craft gin, agave spirits, or a crisp highball—and enjoy riverside views in a relaxed, local vibe.  
Fast, affordable, and no reservation needed—perfect for before dinner or between sightseeing.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Walk up to the counter and pick 2–3 pours (whisky, gin, or themed flight) or go classic with a highball  
- **Step 2**: Chat with the bartender for simple recommendations—dry, smoky, or sweet styles  
- **Step 3**: Sip riverside at the standing ledge and people-watch along Shibuya Stream  
- **Step 4 (Optional)**: Try a final pour—mezcal, Japanese craft spirits, or a limited bottle before you roll on  

## What's Included

- 30–60 min self-guided **standing bar experience**  
- Choice of spirits by the glass (pay-as-you-go)  
- Casual **riverside spot** with breeze and city views  

## Perfect For

- Travelers wanting an **authentic Tokyo bar stop** without spending the whole evening  
- Couples or friends looking for a **pre-dinner apéro** or post-shopping drink  
- Spirits fans exploring **Japanese whisky and highballs** in their natural setting
