## Quick Overview

Try one of Japan's most beloved summer sweets—**kakigori (shaved ice)**—reinvented with premium fruits and handmade syrups.  
Famous in Kawagoe, **"Kore ga Kakigori"** now brings its fluffy, head-ache-free ice to **Shibuya Scramble Square**.  
In this **30-minute indulgence**, enjoy seasonal creations like *Fresh Strawberry Milk* or *Melon Cream* paired with warming Taiwanese tea.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Choose your kakigori from seasonal flavors — *strawberry, melon, mango, peach, matcha*, and more  
- **Step 2**: Taste the difference of ultra-pure ice that doesn't cause "ice headaches"  
- **Step 3**: Enjoy housemade syrups and creams made from real fruits — no artificial flavors  
- **Step 4**: Pair your dessert with **Taiwanese tea** (refillable for ¥300) for perfect flavor balance  
- **Step 5**: Capture **Instagram-worthy moments** of colorful, mountain-like shaved ice bowls  

## What's Included

- 1 handmade kakigori dessert (seasonal or classic flavor)  
- Housemade syrup and toppings (fruit, cream, beans, etc.)  
- Optional Taiwanese tea pairing (refillable)  
- Seating inside Shibuya Scramble Square B2F (direct station access)  

## Perfect For

- Sweet lovers and Instagram hunters seeking the ultimate dessert shot  
- Couples or friends sharing **seasonal Japanese fruit experiences**  
- Families with kids — fun, safe, and delicious for all ages
