## Quick Overview

Meet irresistibly cute hedgehogs and their tiny friends (guinea pigs, hamsters, rats—and sometimes chinchillas, sugar gliders, degus) at a cozy, low-light "night safari" lounge near Ikebukuro Station. Choose 30 or 60 minutes, add an optional snack cup, and enjoy gentle, hands-on time. Staff keep animal welfare first; no flash or video.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in**, store small items, and get a quick handling briefing
- **Settle into the softly lit lounge** and meet several small animals at a relaxed pace
- **(Optional) Hand-feed** a safe snack to hedgehogs or others—great for photos (no flash)
- **Wrap up** with hand sanitation and a takeaway care tip sheet

## What's Included

- Entry for 30 min or 60 min self-guided animal time
- Use of blankets/gloves on request (for comfort & safety)
- On-site guidance by staff (welfare-first handling)
- Optional add-on: Animal snack set ¥300 per cup (paid on site)

## Perfect For

- Solo travelers, couples, and families seeking a calm, cute, rain-proof break on the Yamanote Line
- Animal lovers wanting a unique small-animal encounter
- Travelers looking for a relaxing 30–60 minute activity in Ikebukuro
