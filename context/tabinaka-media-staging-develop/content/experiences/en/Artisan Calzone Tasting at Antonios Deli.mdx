# Taste an Artisan Calzone at Antonio's Deli

## Quick Overview

Savor a freshly baked Calzone crafted from an authentic Italian recipe at Antonio's Deli, established in 1944 by Antonio Cancemi. This quick and delicious snack offers a taste of Italy right in the heart of Shibuya's Tokyu Food Show.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Order your Calzone at the deli counter.
- **Step 2**: Watch as it's warmed to perfection in the oven.
- **Step 3**: Bite into the golden, crispy crust to reveal the savory, melted cheese and filling inside.

## What's Included

- One freshly baked Calzone

## Perfect For

- Travelers seeking authentic Italian flavors in Tokyo
- Foodies exploring Shibuya Tokyu Food Show
- Visitors needing a quick yet satisfying snack
- Solo travelers or small groups looking to share a portable treat
