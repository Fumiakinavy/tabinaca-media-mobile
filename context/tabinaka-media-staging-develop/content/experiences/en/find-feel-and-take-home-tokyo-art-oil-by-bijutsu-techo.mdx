Browse a **rotating gallery** and curated shop of **artworks, editions, books, and artist-made goods**—all approachable, authentic, and giftable.  
After its **June 2025 renovation**, the store now highlights **creator collaborations and small editions**, making art discovery fast, friendly, and inspiring.
