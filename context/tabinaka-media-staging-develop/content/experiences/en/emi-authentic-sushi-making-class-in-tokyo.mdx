## Quick Overview

Join an intimate, hands-on Edo-style sushi workshop led by a chef with Michelin-starred restaurant experience. Learn professional knife skills, master rice and seasoning balance, and craft beautiful nigiri, maki, and gunkan—all in a small bilingual class for personalized guidance.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Introduction & brief history of Edo-mae sushi  
- **Step 2**: Fun sushi quiz (small prizes)  
- **Step 3**: Chef's lecture & live demonstration (knife skills, rice, seasoning)  
- **Step 4**: Make your own sushi (nigiri, maki, gunkan) with chef support  
- **Step 5**: Photo time & enjoy the sushi you made

## What's Included

- Bilingual instruction (Japanese / English)  
- Quiz prizes (small gifts)
