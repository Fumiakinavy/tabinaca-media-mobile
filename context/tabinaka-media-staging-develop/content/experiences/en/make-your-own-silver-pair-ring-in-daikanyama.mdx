## Quick Overview

Craft a custom silver ring at icci Daikanyama—choose your width (2–10 mm), shape and texture, then finish and take it home the same day. Sessions run about 1 hour; pricing starts from ¥12,000 per ring. Optional birthstones/diamonds can be added (takes extra time for setting).

## What You'll Do

- **Pick your band width** (2–10 mm) and basic profile (round or flat)
- **Select a surface finish**: mirror, matte, hammered, or "stardust"
- **Size, shape, file, and polish** your ring with studio tools under staff guidance
- **(Optional) Choose a stone-setting plan**; rings with stones are kept for setting and collected later
- **Leave with your finished silver ring** the same day (no stone)

## What's Included

- 60-minute workshop slot and same-day completion for silver bands
- Use of tools, materials, and finishing support
- Choice of width & texture
- Basic English assistance from staff
- Gift pouch/packaging for your ring

## Perfect For

- Couples or friends making matching bands or souvenirs
- Solo travelers seeking a hands-on, one-hour creative break near Shibuya
- Last-minute personalized gifts you can bring home immediately
