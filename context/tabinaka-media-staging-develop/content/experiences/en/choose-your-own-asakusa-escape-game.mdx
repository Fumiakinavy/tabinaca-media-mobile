## Quick Overview

Pick one escape game in Asakusa—either at REAL ESCAPE GAME CROSSING Asakusa or NAZOHOUSE Asakusa. Staff give a brief English/Japanese intro, then it's all you and your team solving puzzles in a themed room. Games run 60–90 minutes and are perfect between temple strolls and dinner.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in** at your chosen venue in Asakusa and hear a short rules/story briefing (EN/JP)
- **Enter the room** and work as a team to crack codes, open locks, and trigger mechanisms before time runs out
- **Take a celebratory photo** at the end (win or lose!)

## What's Included

- 1 game admission (choose at booking)
- Pre-game briefing (English/Japanese at CROSSING)
- Puzzle props and game facilitation

## Perfect For

- Friends, couples, families (age 10+ recommended), team bonding, and puzzle lovers who want a time-boxed activity near Senso-ji
- Rainy-day entertainment
- Team-building or group celebrations
