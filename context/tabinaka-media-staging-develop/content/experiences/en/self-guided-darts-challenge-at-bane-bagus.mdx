## Quick Overview

Play like a local at Shibuya's late-night darts hub. Reserve a DARTSLIVE board, choose your favorite games (501, Cricket, etc.), and go head-to-head with friends. Time-charged, walk-in friendly, and open until 5:00 AM. From ¥330 per person (one-drink order required).

## What You'll Do

- **Check in** at Bane BAGUS (Shibuya, Miyamasuzaka) and get your board assignment. Staff set-up support included
- **Warm up**, then play unlimited rounds on your reserved DARTSLIVE board for 30–90 minutes. The venue has 24 machines, including DARTSLIVE3
- **Try popular match modes** (501/701, Cricket) or casual party games—self-guided, no host required
- **Optional**: upgrade to a private "luxury room" (darts + karaoke) for a VIP vibe

## What's Included

- Time-charged access to a DARTSLIVE board (24 boards on site, incl. DARTSLIVE3)
- House rules & basic set-up support from staff
- One drink per guest (venue policy)

## Perfect For

- Friends or couples looking for a quick, active break near Shibuya Station
- Small groups wanting a competitive, photo-friendly activity before/after dinner
- Night-owls—doors stay open until 5:00 AM daily

## Need-to-Know

- **Price guide**: Darts from ¥330 per person (time-charge, tax-in). Private rooms and other games (billiards, ping-pong, sim golf) available at additional cost. One-drink order required per person
- **Hours**: Mon–Fri & eves of holidays 15:00–05:00; Sat/Sun/holidays 14:00–05:00
- **Access**: Kaleido Shibuya Miyamasuzaka B1–B2F, 1-12-1 Shibuya, Shibuya-ku (3 min from Shibuya Station Miyamasuzaka exit)
- **Payment**: Major credit cards, transport IC, e-money, and QR (incl. PayPay, Alipay, WeChat Pay)
- **Suggested Time Options**:
  - 30 min "Quick Throw" — perfect filler between plans
  - 60 min "Shibuya Match Play" — best for 2–4 players
  - 90 min "Mini League" — rotate teams & crown a champ
- **How to Book**: Same-day: Walk in and request darts (subject to wait time). In advance: Reserve online (TableCheck) or call 03-5778-9962. Ask for a darts board or a private darts room
