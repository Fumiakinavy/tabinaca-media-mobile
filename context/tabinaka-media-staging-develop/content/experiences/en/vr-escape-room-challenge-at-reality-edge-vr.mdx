## Quick Overview

Tackle a cinematic VR escape room with your crew in a 75-minute self-guided session near Akihabara. Choose from blockbuster titles (e.g., Escape the Lost Pyramid, Beyond Medusa's Gate) or original adventures. Staff give a short English briefing; gameplay is cooperative and intuitive.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in** at the Kanda studio; gear up and learn basics (briefing + tutorial). Total slot is 75 min; game time is up to 50 min
- **Team up** (2–4 players per mission) and solve multi-room VR puzzles—climb, search, and communicate to escape in time
- **Snap a victory photo**; larger groups can rotate teams (PS5/board games available while waiting)

## What's Included

- Private VR booth + headset and controllers
- Safety briefing & on-site assistance (English briefing available)
- Choice of mission from the current lineup (Assassin's Creed VR titles and more)

## Perfect For

- Friends, couples, and teams who love puzzles and "wow" moments
- Gamers and first-timers alike (experiences designed to minimize motion sickness)
- Groups seeking cutting-edge entertainment in Tokyo
