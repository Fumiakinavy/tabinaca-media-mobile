## Quick Overview

Sip three small pours of KIUCHI Whisky while seated among copper stills at Hitachino Brewing Tokyo Distillery in SEEKBASE (under the JR tracks by Akihabara). Staff give a brief English intro; you compare aromas and flavors at your own pace. Optional add-ons: a gin sample, highball, or a Hitachino Nest beer on tap.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in** at the counter and get a quick orientation (basic English)
- **Choose a 3-whisky tasting flight** or let staff pick by flavor (light → rich)
- **Follow a simple self-tasting routine**: nose → first sip → water → revisit & compare
- **(Optional) Order a highball**, gin/liqueur sample, or Hitachino beer to contrast styles
- **Take a photo** with the stills and note your favorite for souvenirs

## What's Included

- Seat at the distillery bar (amid the stills)
- 3-pour KIUCHI Whisky flight (small pours)
- Water and basic English assistance from staff
- Extras like highballs, gin, beer, and food are pay-as-you-go

## Perfect For

- Whisky-curious travelers, couples, solo sippers
- Craft-beer fans exploring Japanese distillery culture
- Anyone needing a flavorful 45–60 min stop near Akihabara
