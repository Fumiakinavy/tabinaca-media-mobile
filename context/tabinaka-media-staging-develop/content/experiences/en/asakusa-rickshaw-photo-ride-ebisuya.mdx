## Quick Overview

Glide through old-Tokyo in a hand-pulled rickshaw starting at the iconic Kaminarimon Gate. Choose a 30- or 60-minute loop past Sensō-ji, Nakamise, and quiet backstreets while your English-friendly rickshaw puller handles the route and helps capture great photos on your phone.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in** at the Ebisuya Rickshaw stand by Kaminarimon Gate
- **Hop in your private rickshaw** and roll past Nakamise-dori, temple gates, and atmospheric lanes
- **Stop for quick photos** with Kaminarimon, the Five-storied Pagoda, or Skytree views (time permitting)
- **Finish back** at Kaminarimon or request a nearby drop-off

## What's Included

- Private rickshaw (typically seats two adults per vehicle) for 30 or 60 minutes
- English-friendly puller/driver who shares basics and helps take photos

## Perfect For

- First-time visitors, couples, families, solo travelers, and anyone short on time who wants classic Asakusa shots without the crowds on foot
- Weather-flexible touring (operates in fair/light weather)
- Unique photo opportunities with iconic Tokyo backdrops
