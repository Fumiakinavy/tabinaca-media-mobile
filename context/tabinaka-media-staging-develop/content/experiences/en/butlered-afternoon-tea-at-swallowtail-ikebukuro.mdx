## Quick Overview

Step into a neo-Victorian tea salon where professional butlers host you for a 100-minute "at-home" service. Book a seat, choose a set menu and drink, and enjoy graceful, old-world etiquette. English guidance/menu available; reservations required.

## What You'll Do

- **Be welcomed** at the door and escorted to your table (staggered entry by reservation)
- **Follow a short etiquette briefing** (phones away, no photography) and settle in
- **Select one set menu** per guest (comes with one drink) and savor attentive butler service
- **Enjoy unhurried conversation** and tea; staff can assist in basic English
- **Conclude with tableside checkout** before your 100-minute slot ends

## What's Included

- Reserved 100-minute seating in the salon
- Butler hospitality & brief etiquette guidance (basic EN support)
- English menu on request
- Food/drinks purchased on site (one set menu per person required)

## Perfect For

- Travelers seeking a uniquely Japanese butler-café experience
- Couples/friends celebrating
- Fans of themed hospitality and afternoon tea
