## Quick Overview

Just steps from **Shibuya Station**, **SUSHIBUYA** pairs playful Edo-inspired "snackable" sushi with drinks in a stylish café-bar under the **Shibuya Stream** overpass.  
Stay for 60–90 minutes, order mix-and-match plates, and sip **sours or highballs** till late—open as late as **2:00 AM** on weekdays.  
English menus make it easy for travelers to enjoy a **casual, photogenic sushi night**.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Start with a house special—*"mango-cut" tuna or salmon*, or creative rolls like *Salmon Mont Blanc* with cream cheese  
- **Step 2**: Build your own mini crawl: share 4–6 plates of **yakumi nigiri**, rolls, and sides with drinks  
- **Step 3**: Pair with **sours, highballs, or beer**—and look for time-limited "39-wari" drink promos (¥399 each; excl. Fridays)  
- **Step 4**: Enjoy the laid-back vibe under the tracks—perfect for chatting, people-watching, or hopping to the next bar  

## What's Included

- 60–90 min **self-guided sushi & drinks session** (pay-as-you-go)  
- **English menu / tablet ordering** with takeout option  
- Optional **table reservations** (walk-ins welcome early evening)  

## Perfect For

- Friends or couples wanting a **fun, casual sushi night**  
- Travelers craving **late-night bites near Shibuya Station**  
- Content creators looking for **Instagrammable plates** without fine-dining formality  

## Need-to-Know

- **Location:** Shibuya Stream 1F, along Route 246 underpass (~3 min from Shibuya Station)  
- **Hours:**  
  - Weekdays 17:00–26:00 (L.O. food 25:00 / drink 25:30)  
  - Saturday 12:00–25:00  
  - Sunday & Holidays 12:00–23:00  
- **Seats:** ~54; Reservations OK  
- **Budget:** Lunch ¥1,000–2,000 / Dinner ¥3,000–4,000 (courses from ¥5,000 w/ free-flow)  
- **Payments:** Cards and e-payments accepted; non-smoking space
