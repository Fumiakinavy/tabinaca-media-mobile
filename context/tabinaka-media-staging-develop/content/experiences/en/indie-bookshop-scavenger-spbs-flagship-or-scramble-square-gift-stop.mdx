## Quick Overview

Step into **Shibuya Publishing & Booksellers (SPBS)**—Tokyo's cult indie bookstore bridging literature, art, and lifestyle.  
Choose between the **Flagship store in Oku-Shibuya** (a quiet, zine-filled haven) or **+SPBS TOKYO** inside **Shibuya Scramble Square**, a sleek "book & gift" concept perfect for fast yet meaningful souvenirs.  
Each location celebrates creativity and curation, from indie magazines to Japan-made design goods.

## What You'll Do

- **Flagship (Kamiyamacho):** Browse **independent zines, design/photo books**, and staff-curated shelves in a calm, bookish neighborhood.  
- **+SPBS TOKYO (Scramble Square 2F):** Explore ~1,500 items—books, stationery, aroma goods, and accessories—crafted for thoughtful gift-giving.  
- **Mini Challenge:** Find one Japan-made item + one print piece that tells a Tokyo story, then snap your haul in a cozy reading corner or mall atrium.  

## What's Included

- Self-guided exploration of either store  
- English-friendly signage and staff assistance  
- Access to tax-free service at Scramble Square (3F counter; conditions apply)  

## Perfect For

- **Design lovers** and **magazine collectors** seeking authentic Tokyo creativity  
- **Travelers** wanting compact, thoughtful souvenirs  
- **Readers and creators** who enjoy curated independent shops  

## Need-to-Know

- **Flagship (SPBS 本店):** 11:00–21:00 / 17-3 Kamiyamacho / Tel: 03-5465-0588  
- **+SPBS TOKYO:** 10:00–21:00 / Shibuya Scramble Square 2F / Tel: 03-6452-6211  
- **Access:** Scramble Square store is **station-direct**; flagship is **10 min from Shibuya or Yoyogi-Koen**  
- **Photography:** Not allowed inside flagship store  
- **Tax-Free:** Available only at +SPBS TOKYO (building counter, 3F)
