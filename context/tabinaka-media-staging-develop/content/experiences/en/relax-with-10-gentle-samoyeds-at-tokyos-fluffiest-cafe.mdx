## Quick Overview

Step away from the busy streets of Harajuku and enter **Samoyed Café moffu**, your "second home" filled with fluffy white dogs and warm smiles. Spend **30–60 minutes** surrounded by friendly Samoyeds, petting, taking photos, and enjoying the ultimate Tokyo-style animal café experience.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Enter the cozy café and meet up to **10 Samoyeds**, all friendly and eager for attention  
- **Step 2**: Pet, cuddle, and take photos with the dogs in a relaxed, living room-like space  
- **Step 3**: Learn about the Samoyed breed — their history, personality, and why they're known as *"smiling dogs"*  
- **Step 4**: Order a drink and enjoy your time while the dogs roam freely around you  
- **Step 5 (Optional)**: Shop for Samoyed-themed goods or follow the café's Instagram to stay connected  

## What's Included

- 30–60 min Samoyed café session  
- Interaction with up to 10 Samoyeds  
- One drink (coffee, tea, or soft drink)  
- Staff assistance (basic English support)  
- Free time for photos and play  

## Perfect For

- Dog lovers seeking a unique Tokyo memory  
- Couples and friends wanting a heartwarming break during shopping in Harajuku  
- Solo travelers who miss their pets at home  
- Anyone in need of fluffy comfort and smiles
