## Quick Overview

Dive into **Shibuya Yokocho**, a 100-meter indoor/outdoor alley at **RAYARD MIYASHITA PARK** featuring **19 eateries** serving local dishes from **Hokkaido to Okinawa**.  
Enjoy a self-guided street-food crawl with rotating entertainment—**Bon-odori, DJs, enka nights, and anime collaborations**—in one of Shibuya's most vibrant food hangouts.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Choose 3 regions (e.g., Hokkaido, Tohoku, Okinawa) and order a small plate at each stall—ramen, gyoza, yakitori, or regional specialties  
- **Step 2**: Add a drink (sour, highball, beer, or soft drink) and stroll through the alley or terrace tables  
- **Step 3**: Catch live or seasonal entertainment—Bon-odori, DJ sets, or themed events  
- **Step 4**: Snap the neon—lantern-lit storefronts under the MIYASHITA PARK arches make for perfect night photos  

## What's Included

- Self-guided tasting route inside one lively venue (pay-as-you-go)  
- Access to 19 eateries representing regions across Japan  
- Option to enjoy both **indoor and terrace seating** (~1,500 total seats)  
- No reservation needed—come and go freely within the venue  

## Perfect For

- Travelers wanting to sample **Japanese food culture from north to south** in one stop  
- Friends or couples planning a **lively pre-game or late-night food adventure**  
- Families or mixed groups who want **many choices under one roof**  
- Anyone who loves **festival energy** and casual street dining in the heart of Tokyo  

## Need-to-Know

- **Location:** RAYARD MIYASHITA PARK South 1F, 6-20-10 Jingumae, Shibuya  
- **Hours:** Mon–Sat & Holidays 11:00–05:00, Sun 11:00–23:00 (individual stalls vary)  
- **Scale:** ~100 meters long, 19 stores, ~1,500 seats  
- **Vibe:** Food + entertainment — *every day feels like a festival*
