## Quick Overview

Meet and gently feed fluffy rabbits at a long-running Ikebukuro animal café. Staff provide simple English guidance, you sanitize, choose your course, and enjoy calm one-on-one time with the bunnies (otter course/upgrade available at select times). Walk-ins are welcome; reservations recommended on weekends/holidays.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in**, sanitize hands, and review house rules (no lifting, quiet voices, no flash)
- **Choose your course**; receive a small cup of approved rabbit feed
- **Spend 30–60 minutes** relaxing, petting, and feeding rabbits in a seated area
- **(Optional) Book a rabbit + otter slot** if available for safe viewing/feeding of Asian small-clawed otters

## What's Included

- Entry for the selected time slot
- 1 cup of rabbit feed per person (rabbit courses)
- Basic English support from staff

## Perfect For

- Couples, friends, and families looking for a soothing, photo-friendly break
- Animal lovers who prefer a short, gentle, low-language-barrier activity
- Travelers seeking a unique small-animal experience in Tokyo
