## Quick Overview

Step into Tokyo's most serene, all-white owl salon for a 60-minute self-guided encounter. Staff give a short English safety briefing, then you'll photograph, gently touch, and (if the owl agrees) hand-perch one under supervision. A pro snapshot and printed keepsake postcard are included. Advance online booking only.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in** and hear the English do's & don'ts for interacting with owls
- **Meet several species** up close; take unlimited photos (no flash)
- **Gently touch** and, when suitable, hand-perch an owl with staff assisting
- **Receive a professional photo** and a printed owl-postcard souvenir before you leave

## What's Included

- 60-minute session (small group)
- English guidance on handling & safety
- Supervised touch/hand-perch experience
- Pro photo & printed postcard keepsake

## Perfect For

- Animal lovers, couples, small groups, and photographers seeking a calm, uniquely Japanese experience in Akihabara
- Travelers wanting a serene break from shopping and gaming
- Anyone seeking memorable wildlife photos
