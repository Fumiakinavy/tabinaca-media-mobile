## Quick Overview

Unwind with friendly cats right in central Shibuya. Drop in for 30–90 minutes, sip unlimited soft drinks, time your visit for the adorable "meal time," and (optionally) treat the kitties. Two locations within walking distance of Shibuya Station—pick the vibe that fits your plans. No reservation needed.

## What You'll Do

- **Settle into a sunny lounge** and meet the resident cats; relax, read, or take photos (no flash)
- **Time it for "Meal Time"** (10:30 & 19:00) to see everyone line up and munch together—peak cuteness
- **Enjoy the included drink bar** throughout your stay
- **(Optional) Buy cat treats** on site to make new furry friends (¥550)

## What's Included

- Entry for your chosen time (30–90 minutes, self-guided)
- Unlimited soft-drink bar
- Wi-Fi / comfy seating; workspace-friendly zones at some tables

## Perfect For

- Solo travelers needing a calm reset between Shibuya sights
- Couples & friends looking for a cozy photo-worthy stop
- Cat lovers and light-schedule "gap-time" plans (30–60 min)

## Need-to-Know

- **Pick Your Location** (both open 10:00–21:00; last entry 20:30):
  - **1) MOCHA Shibuya Center-gai** (5 min from Hachiko Exit): Asortie Shibuya 8F, 32-12 Udagawa-cho. Family-friendly; children 3+ welcomed with a guardian (limits apply). No outside food; drinks OK
  - **2) MOCHA Lounge Shibuya Koen-dori** (calmer lounge vibe): Kokusai Bldg. 7F, 33-1 Udagawa-cho. Ages 13+ only. Also close to the station; workspace-friendly seating
- **Pricing** (drink bar included; tax-in prices in parentheses):
  - Center-gai: First 30 min ¥1,080 (¥1,188) on weekdays / ¥1,380 (¥1,518) weekends & holidays; then ¥200 (¥220) per 10 min; weekday max ¥3,200 (¥3,520), holiday max ¥3,800 (¥4,180)
  - Koen-dori: First 30 min ¥1,200 (¥1,320) on weekdays / ¥1,500 (¥1,650) weekends & holidays; then ¥200 (¥220) per 10 min; weekday max ¥3,200 (¥3,520), holiday max ¥3,800 (¥4,180)
- **Suggested Time Slots**:
  - Quick Purr (30–45 min): Pop in between shopping stops; great for a recharge
  - Standard Lounge (60 min): Arrive 10:25 or 18:55 to catch "Meal Time"
  - Deep Unwind (90 min): Settle in with a book and rotate between play and chill
- **Good to Know**: Rules: Be gentle; don't chase or pick up cats; no flash; no outside food (drinks OK). Ages: Center-gai welcomes kids 3+ with a 20+ guardian (max 3 kids per guardian). Koen-dori is 13+ only. Access & Hours: Both sites are a short walk from Shibuya Station; open daily 10:00–21:00 (last entry 20:30). Walk-ins welcome. Add-ons: Limited-quantity treats (¥550) sell out—ask at check-in
