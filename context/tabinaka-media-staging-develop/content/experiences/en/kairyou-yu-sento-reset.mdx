## Quick Overview

Unplug just 10 minutes from Shibuya Crossing at a century-old public bath reborn sleek and modern. Rotate through a carbonated hot bath, cold plunge, and (optional) far-infrared sauna for a fast, deeply relaxing reset. Walk-in friendly, tattoos accepted, English guidance at check-in.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in**: Store shoes, purchase entry (¥550) and optional sauna add-on (+¥450); rent towels if needed
- **Wash & warm up**: Shower seated at the wash stations (soap/shampoo available); hydrate
- **Soak: Carbonated Bath**: Fine bubbles in soft water ease muscle tension and jet lag
- **Cool Down: Cold Bath**: A quick plunge to refresh circulation
- **Optional Sauna Cycle**: Far-infrared dry sauna, cool rinse, brief rest. Repeat as time allows
- **Finish**: Rinse, dry, moisturize; coin laundry on site if needed

## What's Included

- Soft-water baths (hot carbonated bath, medium-temp bath, cold bath)
- Far-infrared sauna (+¥450)
- Amenities: body soap & shampoo; towel rental available; hair dryers (¥20/3 min)
- Coin laundry (8:00–24:30)

## Perfect For

- Travelers with 1 hour to recharge between Shibuya and Ebisu
- Runners, shoppers, and night-owls needing a quick reset
- Guests with tattoos seeking a welcoming sento experience
