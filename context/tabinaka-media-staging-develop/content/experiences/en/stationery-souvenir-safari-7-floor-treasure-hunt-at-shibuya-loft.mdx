## Quick Overview

Just a few steps from **Shibuya's Scramble Crossing**, **Shibuya LOFT** is a **seven-floor lifestyle emporium**—the go-to destination for stationery, design gifts, J-beauty, and homeware.  
Open daily from **11:00–21:00**, it offers **tax-free shopping**, English-friendly signs, and endless photo-worthy aisles, perfect for a quick souvenir hunt or rainy-day plan.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1:** Pick your focus—stationery, souvenirs, or design gifts. Grab a basket and floor map at the entrance.  
- **Step 2:** Explore B1F for **Japan's top stationery brands**, then climb floor by floor for homeware, beauty, and travel goods.  
- **Step 3:** Compare best-sellers and local finds—staff can point you to trending products.  
- **Step 4:** Complete purchases and, if eligible, head to the **tax-free counter** (passport required).  
- **Step 5:** Leave with curated souvenirs and Shibuya-exclusive designs.  

## What's Included

- Self-guided access to all seven floors  
- English-friendly signage  
- Optional **tax-free refund service** (bring passport)
