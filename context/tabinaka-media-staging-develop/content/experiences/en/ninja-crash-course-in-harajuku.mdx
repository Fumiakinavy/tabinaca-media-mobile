## Quick Overview

A fast, indoor ninja training session (about 45–60 minutes total, core training ~30–45) where you suit up in an authentic ninja outfit and learn three classic skills—shuriken (throwing stars), blowgun, and safe replica katana—guided by English-speaking instructors. Great in any weather; optional café food & drinks available.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in, change into a ninja costume**, and get a quick safety briefing
- **Learn proper stance and technique**, then throw real shuriken at targets
- **Try a traditional blowgun** (fukiya) with supervised target practice
- **Practice basic katana forms** with a safe replica sword and capture epic action photos
- **Wrap up with free time** for photos/videos; grab themed drinks or snacks at the café

## What's Included

- Ninja costume rental (use during the session)
- Instructor-led training in shuriken, blowgun, and katana basics (in English/Japanese)
- Use of all practice weapons, targets, and props; plenty of photo time indoors

## Perfect For

- Friends, couples, and families seeking a unique, hands-on Tokyo memory
- Content creators who want dynamic, Instagrammable action shots
- Solo travelers and small groups looking for weather-proof activity in Harajuku
