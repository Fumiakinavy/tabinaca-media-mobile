## Quick Overview

At Cagen Sushi in the Shibuya Tokyu Food Show, savor expertly crafted Edomae-style nigiri alongside New York–inspired rolls for a perfect blend of tradition and innovation.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Choose your set:
  - **Tuna Assortment** (900 ¥): A selection of fresh tuna nigiri.
  - **Toro & Takuan Maki Set** (600 ¥): Rich fatty tuna rolled with pickled daikon radish.
- **Step 2**: Watch skilled sushi chefs prepare each piece to order, ensuring peak freshness and flavor.
- **Step 3**: Enjoy your sushi on-site with a complimentary tasting of the shop’s signature condiments.

## What's Included

- Your choice of Tuna Assortment or Toro & Takuan Maki Set

## Perfect For

- Sushi enthusiasts craving classic Edomae flavors
- Foodies seeking a quick, high-quality bite
- Small groups sharing a sampler menu
- Visitors curious about traditional meets modern sushi
