## Quick Overview

Slip into a beautifully styled kimono at Asakusa Kimono Rental YAE, then head out on a short, self-guided photo stroll to Sensō-ji and Nakamise Street—just minutes away. Prices are simple (hair set & accessories included; no add-on upsells) and staff can help in English/Chinese.

## What You'll Do

- **Check in at YAE** (3F, Hanakawado 1-2-6). Pick your kimono/yukata from classic to retro styles
- **Staff will dress you** and do a quick hair set (about 20–30 min)
- **Head out on a self-guided loop**: Kaminarimon Gate → Nakamise shopping street → Sensō-ji Main Hall/5-story Pagoda
- **Snap portraits** at each spot; return the kimono by closing time (17:30)

## What's Included

- Kimono/yukata set (kimono, obi, basic accessories) with professional hair styling
- Fitting by experienced staff with English/Chinese/Japanese support
- Simple, all-in pricing (hair & accessories already included)

## Perfect For

- Solo travelers, couples, and friends wanting iconic Tokyo photos in under 90 minutes
- First-timers (multilingual staff + easy walk to landmarks)
- Rain-or-shine plans before/after Tokyo Skytree or Ueno
