## Quick Overview

Located on **Shibuya Stream's 3rd floor**, **SUSHI TOKYO TEN** offers a refined yet relaxed **Edomae-style omakase** led by skilled chefs right at the counter.  
Each course alternates between **tsumami (small plates)** and **nigiri**, showcasing **seasonal fish**, classic **curing**, and **aging techniques**—crafted in a modern, approachable setting directly connected to Shibuya Station.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1:** Take your seat at the **chef's counter** (or limited table seats)  
- **Step 2:** Begin your omakase journey—watch as each **nigiri and appetizer** is served in rhythm  
- **Step 3:** Pair with **sake or Japanese drinks** (pay-as-you-go)  
- **Step 4:** Choose your experience:  
  - **Lunch Omakase** ¥4,950 – lighter and faster  
  - **Dinner Omakase** ¥9,900 – expanded seasonal lineup  
- **Step 5:** Enjoy 60–90 minutes of calm craftsmanship amid Shibuya's buzz  

## What's Included

- Chef's **omakase course** (lunch or dinner)  
- Counter or table seating (~28 seats total)  
- English menu terminology; simple staff explanations available  

## Perfect For

- **Travelers** seeking a true Edomae experience without high-end formality  
- **Couples or friends** planning a special meal right in central Shibuya  
- **Sushi fans** curious about traditional curing and aging methods  

## Practical Info

- **Location:** Shibuya Stream 3F (connected to Shibuya Station, C2 exit)  
- **Hours:** Lunch 11:00–15:00 (L.O. 14:00) / Dinner 17:00–23:00 (L.O. 21:30)  
- **Price:** Lunch ¥4,950 / Dinner ¥9,900  
- **Payment:** Cashless only (credit cards & smartphone pay from Apr 2025)  
- **Booking:** Reserve online via TableCheck or walk in off-peak  
- **Seats:** Approx. 28 (mostly counter; reservations recommended)
