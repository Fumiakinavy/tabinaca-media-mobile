## Quick Overview

Spend **90 minutes to 2 hours (or more)** at **Bunkashinka**, a three-story **culture hub** in the heart of Shibuya where food, art, nightlife, and wellness collide. Enjoy bold *Neo-Chinese* flavors, soak in sauna steam, and retreat to stylish lodging if you miss the last train. It's a perfect blend of **hedonism and relaxation**, redefining what a night in Tokyo can be.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Start with a **Neo-Chinese dinner** — shareable signature dishes like *gyoza*, *mapo tofu*, and *lu rou fan* in a neon-lit setting filled with art, music, and conversation  
- **Step 2**: Move to the **sauna & relaxation area** — unwind in steam, sauna, and quiet lounge zones designed for mental reset  
- **Step 3 (Optional)**: Stay the night — check into the on-site **hostel lodging** with cozy beds if you'd rather not rush for the last train  
- **Step 4**: Enjoy the **late-night vibe** — standing bar, drink pairings, occasional live performances, and curated playlists representing Tokyo's creative scene  

## What's Included

- Entry to Neo-Chinese restaurant (dinner) + shared dishes  
- Access to the sauna / wellness area  
- (Optional) Overnight stay in hostel lodging  
- Basic English hospitality support; English menus or photo menus available

- Travelers who love immersive local culture, nightlife, and rest in one spot  
- Foodies eager to try inventive **Chinese-inspired Tokyo fusion dishes**
