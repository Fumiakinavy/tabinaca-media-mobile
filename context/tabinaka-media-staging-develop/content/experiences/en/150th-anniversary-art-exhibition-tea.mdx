## Quick Overview

Visit the Yamatane Museum of Art’s special 150th Anniversary Exhibition “Uemura Shoen and Splendid Women” (May 17–July 27) and relax with traditional Japanese sweets and matcha in the on-site café.

## What You'll Do

- **Step 1**: Admire modern and contemporary Japanese-style paintings in the special exhibition.
- **Step 2**: Stroll through the museum’s permanent collection galleries at your own pace.
- **Step 3**: Head to the café to savor traditional wagashi and a freshly whisked bowl of matcha.

## What's Included

- Admission to the 150th Anniversary Exhibition (Special ticket)
- One serving of traditional Japanese sweets and matcha at the museum café

## Perfect For

- Art enthusiasts interested in Japanese-style paintings
- Culture seekers looking to dive into Japan’s artistic heritage
- Tea lovers craving an authentic matcha & wagashi pairing
- Travelers wanting a calm, reflective museum visit
