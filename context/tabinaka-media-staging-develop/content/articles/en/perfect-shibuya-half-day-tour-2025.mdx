Shibuya. The name conjures images of the world's busiest intersection, neon lights, and infinite choices. But experiencing its essence in limited time can feel daunting. This guide is your **digital concierge**, a foolproof 4-hour plan created by a local friend to eliminate choice anxiety and maximize memorable moments.

---

## Serenity Before the Culture Shock: Meiji Jingu Shrine

**10:00 AM – 11:00 AM**

**Access & Arrival**

- **Station:** Harajuku (JR Yamanote) or Meiji-jingumae (Tokyo Metro)
- **Landmark:** Giant torii gate leading into a forest of 100,000 donated trees

**Ritual Walk & Purification**

1. Purify at the Temizuya (ladle wash)
2. Bow–Clap–Bow at the Main Hall
3. Write an **Ema** wish or draw **Omikuji** fortune

**Pro Tips**

- Grounds: **Free** (museum/Inner Garden extra)
- Best for tranquility before the city pulse
- Weekend weddings are a beautiful bonus

## A Tale of Two Streets: The Harajuku Experience

**11:00 AM – 12:30 PM**

### Takeshita Street: Kawaii Culture

- **Sights & Sounds:** Colorful crepes, giant rainbow cotton candy, cosplay shops
- **Must-Eat:** Instagram-worthy crepes and sweets
- **Shopping:** Teen fashion, accessories, cosplay

### Cat Street: The "Cool" Alternative

- **Vibe:** Laid-back pedestrian lane with select boutiques, vintage finds, chic cafés
- **Ideal For:** Travelers seeking style without the crowds

---

## Grand Finale: Into Shibuya's Iconic Heart

**12:30 PM – 2:00 PM**

### The Walk In

A 15–20 minute Cat Street stroll brings you to Shibuya's bustling center.

### Hachiko Statue

- **Story:** The loyal Akita's vigil for his master
- **Tip:** Best photo early or late to avoid crowds

### The Scramble Crossing: Climax

Up to 3,000 people cross in each light change—a true urban spectacle.

## Shibuya Survival Kit: Your On-Hand Concierge

**Exits & Directions**

- **Hachiko Gate:** Scramble Crossing, 109, Center Gai
- **Central Gate:** Hikarie, Scramble Square
- **South Gate:** Bus & taxi terminal

**Luggage Storage**

- **Coin Lockers:** ¥300–¥900, near every exit & Mark City
- **ecbo Cloak:** App-booked staffed storage

**Connectivity & Power**

- **Wi-Fi:** SHIBUYA CITY Wi-Fi, facility hotspots
- **Charging:** ChargeSPOT everywhere, free boxes at Don Quijote

**Family Facilities**

- **Nursing Rooms:** Hikarie B2, Scramble Square 13F, Mark City 4F

**Cash & Exchange**

- **ATMs:** Seven Bank (7-Eleven), Japan Post Bank
- **Exchange:** Travelex in Mark City
